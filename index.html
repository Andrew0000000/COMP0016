Utilise Powerful AI Services with Little to No Code.

From automated healthcare appointment bookings to preliminary diagnosis

Abstract

The demand for NHS services is at an all-time high facing an aging population and an ever-increasing burden of chronic diseases.

We believe the use of IBM Watson's natural language processing services and Microsoft Power Platform in building custom connectors, non IT-professionals can easily utilise a suite of powerful AI services with little to no code, automating many of the redundant tasks that medical practitioners currently perform manually and allowing them to focus on providing care to their patients. With the use of these technologies, we can automate tasks such as:

  • Patient triage
  • Appointment-scheduling
  • Diagnostic decision-making
